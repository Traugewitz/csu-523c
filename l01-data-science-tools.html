  1. Compute the day-over-day change in discharge for each gauge — how much flow rose or fell compared to the previous day. Store this as a new column called flow_delta. This tells you whether conditions are improving or deteriorating, which is often more actionable than the raw level alone.
#Calculate day over day change
flow_q1 <- raw |>
  arrange(site_no, Date) |>
  group_by(site_no) |>
  mutate(flow_delta = Flow - lag(Flow)) |>
  ungroup()
  1. Filter to my.date and produce two formatted tables using flextable:
flow_today <- flow_q1 |>
  filter(Date == my.date)

top_flow <- flow_today |>
  slice_max(order_by = Flow, n = 5) #selects largest values, keep top 5
